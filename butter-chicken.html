Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Requirements

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Skillet
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Description

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric, and chili powder. Add chicken cubes and marinate for at least 1 hour. This helps to tenderize the chicken and infuse it with flavor.
  2. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until onions are golden brown. This forms the base of the sauce.
  3. Add marinated chicken to the skillet. Cook until chicken is no longer pink. This ensures the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Stir in tomato puree and cook for 10 minutes. This adds a rich tomato flavor to the dish.
  5. Add heavy cream and salt. Simmer for another 10 minutes. The cream adds a rich and creamy texture to the sauce.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan. The cilantro adds a fresh and vibrant flavor to the dish.
