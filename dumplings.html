Ingredients

  • 1 package dumpling wrappers
  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Requirements

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Skillet or wok
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Description

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine ground pork, cabbage, green onions, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and sesame oil. This forms the flavorful filling for the dumplings.
  2. Place a small amount of filling in the center of each dumpling wrapper. Fold the wrapper in half and pinch the edges to seal. This ensures the filling is enclosed.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Fry dumplings until golden brown on the bottom. This adds a crispy texture to the dumplings.
  4. Add a small amount of water to the skillet and cover. Steam dumplings for 5-7 minutes, until filling is cooked through. This ensures the filling is fully cooked.
  5. Remove cover and cook until water evaporates and dumplings are crispy. Serve hot with dipping sauce. The dipping sauce adds an extra layer of flavor.
