Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Pickles
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Ketchup and mustard

Requirements

  • Grill or skillet
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Description

  1. Preheat grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Form ground beef into 4 patties. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook patties on the grill or skillet for 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  4. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  5. Toast hamburger buns on the grill or skillet until golden brown.
  6. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments. Serve hot.
