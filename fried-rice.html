Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Requirements

  • Skillet or wok
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Description

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add onion and garlic, and saut√© for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  3. Push vegetables to the side of the skillet and pour beaten eggs into the center. Scramble the eggs until cooked through.
  4. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, until rice is heated through.
  5. Stir in green onions and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.
