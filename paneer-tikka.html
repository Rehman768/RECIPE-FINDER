Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Requirements

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Skewers
  • Grill or oven
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Description

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric, and chili powder. Add paneer cubes and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Thread marinated paneer, onion, and bell pepper onto skewers.
  3. Preheat grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  4. Brush skewers with oil and grill or bake until paneer is golden brown and vegetables are tender.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with mint chutney.
