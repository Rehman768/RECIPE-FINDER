Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Requirements

  • Large pot
  • Skillet
  • Mixing bowl
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Description

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, cook pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta and garlic. Remove from heat.
  5. Quickly pour egg and cheese mixture over the spaghetti, tossing to coat. The residual heat will cook the eggs and create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
