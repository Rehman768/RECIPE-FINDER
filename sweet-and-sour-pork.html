Sweet and Sour Pork

Ingredients

  • 500g pork tenderloin, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water
  • Salt to taste

Requirements

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Skillet or wok
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, coat pork cubes with cornstarch.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add pork and cook until browned. Remove pork from skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add bell peppers and onion. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add pineapple chunks, ketchup, rice vinegar, soy sauce, and brown sugar. Stir to combine.
  5. Return pork to the skillet. Add cornstarch mixture and cook until sauce thickens.
  6. Season with salt to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.
