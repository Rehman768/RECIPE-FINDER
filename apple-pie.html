Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 2 pie crusts
  • 6 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp butter, cut into small pieces

Requirements

  • Pie dish
  • Mixing bowl
  • Rolling pin
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Description

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). This ensures the oven is hot enough to bake the pie evenly.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss to coat. This mixture forms the filling for the pie.
  3. Roll out one pie crust and fit into a pie dish. Fill with apple mixture and dot with butter. The butter adds richness to the filling.
  4. Roll out the second pie crust and place over the apples. Trim and crimp edges to seal. Cut slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape. This prevents the pie from becoming soggy.
  5. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly. This ensures the crust is cooked through and the filling is hot.
  6. Let cool for at least 2 hours before serving. This allows the filling to set and makes it easier to slice.
