Ingredients

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups brewed espresso, cooled
  • 1/2 cup coffee liqueur
  • 1 package ladyfingers
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting

Requirements

  • Mixing bowls
  • Electric mixer
  • Spatula
  • Baking dish
  • Sifter

Description

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until thick and pale.
  2. Add mascarpone cheese and mix until smooth.
  3. In another bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold into mascarpone mixture.
  4. Combine espresso and coffee liqueur in a shallow dish. Dip each ladyfinger into the mixture and arrange in a single layer in a baking dish.
  5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and remaining mascarpone mixture.
  6. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  7. Before serving, dust with unsweetened cocoa powder using a sifter.
