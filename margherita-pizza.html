Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Requirements

  • Pizza stone or baking sheet
  • Rolling pin
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Description

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Place pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven to heat.
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to desired thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Arrange mozzarella slices on top of the sauce.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
  6. Transfer pizza to the preheated stone or baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil leaves. Serve hot.
