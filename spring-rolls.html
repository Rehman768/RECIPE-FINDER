Spring Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 package spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Requirements

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Skillet or wok
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Description

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine cabbage, carrots, bean sprouts, green onions, garlic, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  2. Place a small amount of filling in the center of each spring roll wrapper. Fold the sides over the filling and roll tightly.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Fry spring rolls until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with dipping sauce.
